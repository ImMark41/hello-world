Sales & Distribution Software

specific Pharmaceutical sales and distribution software can help such verticals as drugs and biotechnology, chemicals, cosmetics, to reduce operational cost while improving customer service and order fulfillment performance. Pharmaceutical sales and distribution software provides you the right tools to efficiently plan, assemble, ship, and deliver the goods to your customers on time. PharmaNET gives the power to streamline and automate the complete sales and distribution function of your business. The solution helps you manage global business with ease, with multi-currency and multi-country features. PharmaNET provides great help to sales manager to optimize the way he enters and post sales orders. Sales manager can split orders across multiple warehouses, split an order into multiple dispatches, allocate inventory, verify credit limit, accept returns, and more. Integrated workflow with notifications and alerts, back order and order status reports, improves serving customers and timely execution of processes ensuring customer satisfaction.
